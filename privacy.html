Privacy Policy

Reliv  ·  Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reliv ("the App," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. Reliv is operated by an individual developer (the "Operator"). By using Reliv, you agree to the practices described here.

What Reliv Does

Reliv is a group photo app. Friends capture photos and videos during a shared event, and the content stays hidden from everyone until a creator-set reveal time. To make this work, the App stores your account information and the media you contribute to events.

Information We Collect

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address (which may be an Apple private relay address). We use this to create and identify your account.
  • Profile information. A display name and username you set within the App.
  • Content you create. Photos and videos you capture, and the events you create or join, including which users are members of an event.
  • Technical and log data. Like any internet service, our infrastructure providers automatically process basic technical information (such as IP address and request timestamps) to deliver and secure the service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create your account and let you sign in.
  • To operate the core features: creating and joining events, storing media, and revealing content at the scheduled time.
  • To display your profile and content to other members of the events you participate in.
  • To maintain the security, reliability, and integrity of the service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your photos or videos for advertising.

Service Providers

Reliv relies on a small number of trusted infrastructure providers to function. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

  • Apple — provides Sign in with Apple for authentication.
  • Supabase — provides the database, authentication, and storage that host your account data and media on our behalf.

These providers process data only to deliver their services to us and are bound by their own privacy and security commitments.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your profile and the content you own, including associated media files, and delete your authentication record. Some technical logs held by our infrastructure providers may persist for a limited period as part of their standard operations.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Delete your account. You can permanently delete your account and associated content at any time from within the App, in the profile settings.
  • Access and correction. You can update your display name and username in the App. For other requests regarding your data, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Reliv is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
